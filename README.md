# CLAUDE RESEARCH EXCELLENCE SYSTEM (CRES)

> *The world's most advanced AI-powered research paper creation framework — built for the top 0.0001%*

---

## What Is CRES?

CRES is a comprehensive, skill-based framework that transforms research ideas into papers that pass peer review at Nature, Science, Cell, NEJM, NeurIPS, ICML, or equivalent venues. It provides structured prompts, adversarial review simulations, and systematic workflows that embody the best practices of the world's most elite researchers.

## Quick Start (5 minutes to first prompt)

1. **Copy `MASTER_PROMPT.md`** — paste as your Claude system prompt
2. **State your research area**: "I'm working on [TOPIC]"
3. **Activate your needed skill**: "ACTIVATE: IDEATION"
4. **Follow the structured output** — iterate from there

## The 10 Skills

| # | Skill | Purpose | Activation |
|---|-------|---------|------------|
| 01 | **Ideation Engine** | Transform vague ideas into razor-sharp research questions | `ACTIVATE: IDEATION` |
| 02 | **Literature Command** | Achieve genuine mastery & synthesis of a literature | `ACTIVATE: LITERATURE` |
| 03 | **Methodology Architect** | Design methodology that pre-empts reviewer critiques | `ACTIVATE: METHODOLOGY` |
| 04 | **Data Intelligence** | Transform raw results into compelling scientific findings | `ACTIVATE: DATA` |
| 05 | **Writing Excellence** | Produce rigorous, clear, compelling scientific prose | `ACTIVATE: WRITING` |
| 06 | **Quality Fortress** | Subject your paper to adversarial review before submission | `ACTIVATE: QUALITY` |
| 07 | **Journal Strategy** | Match paper to venue & craft winning submission materials | `ACTIVATE: JOURNAL` |
| 08 | **Ethics & Compliance** | Ensure research integrity across all dimensions | `ACTIVATE: ETHICS` |
| 09 | **Citation Mastery** | Build a strategic citation architecture | `ACTIVATE: CITATION` |
| 10 | **Impact Amplifier** | Maximize post-publication reach and citations | `ACTIVATE: IMPACT` |

## Skill Combination Matrix

| Task | Skills to Activate |
|------|-------------------|
| Starting from scratch | IDEATION → LITERATURE → METHODOLOGY |
| Have data, need to write | DATA → WRITING → QUALITY |
| Almost ready to submit | QUALITY → JOURNAL → CITATION |
| Just got reviewer comments | QUALITY (revision mode) → JOURNAL |
| Post-acceptance | IMPACT |
| Full paper from idea | All 10 in sequence |

## Philosophy

This system is built on a core conviction: the best research is never produced by a formula — it is produced by a rigorous mind that knows when to follow the rules and when to break them. Use these prompts not as scripts but as frameworks. Adapt them to your domain, your constraints, and your creative instincts.
