# Multi-Format Citation Guide: Comprehensive Reference Formatting for 8 Academic Styles

> **One reference, eight formats. Never struggle with citation formatting again.**

---

## 1. APA 7th Edition

### Journal Article

**One author:**
```
Smith, J. A. (2023). Neural mechanisms of decision-making under
uncertainty. Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience, 35(4), 567–589.
https://doi.org/10.1162/jocn_a_01923
```

**Two authors:**
```
Smith, J. A., & Jones, B. C. (2023). Neural mechanisms of
decision-making under uncertainty. Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience,
35(4), 567–589. https://doi.org/10.1162/jocn_a_01923
```

**Three to twenty authors** (list all):
```
Smith, J. A., Jones, B. C., Lee, D., Garcia, M., & Patel, R.
(2023). Neural mechanisms of decision-making under uncertainty.
Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience, 35(4), 567–589.
https://doi.org/10.1162/jocn_a_01923
```

**Twenty-one or more authors** (first 19, ellipsis, last author):
```
Smith, J. A., Jones, B. C., Lee, D., Garcia, M., Patel, R.,
Chen, W., Kim, S., Müller, H., O'Brien, T., Santos, A., Nakamura,
Y., Anderson, K., Dubois, P., Fernández, L., Gupta, N., Hansen,
E., Ivanov, I., Jensen, J., Kowalski, M., ... Zhang, X. (2023).
Neural mechanisms of decision-making under uncertainty. Journal of
Cognitive Neuroscience, 35(4), 567–589.
https://doi.org/10.1162/jocn_a_01923
```

### Book

**Whole book:**
```
Kahneman, D. (2011). Thinking, fast and slow. Farrar, Straus and
Giroux.
```

**Edited book:**
```
Gazzaniga, M. S. (Ed.). (2020). The cognitive neurosciences (6th
ed.). MIT Press.
```

**Translated book:**
```
Sapolsky, R. M. (2017). Behave: The biology of humans at our best
and worst (S. Translated, Trans.). Penguin Press. (Original work
published 2017)
```

### Book Chapter

```
Smith, J. A. (2023). Decision-making and the prefrontal cortex. In
B. C. Jones & D. Lee (Eds.), Handbook of cognitive neuroscience
(2nd ed., pp. 234–256). Academic Press.
https://doi.org/10.1016/B978-0-12-123456-7.00012-3
```

### Conference Paper (Published Proceedings)

```
Chen, L., & Wang, R. (2023). Attention-based models for medical
image segmentation. In Proceedings of the 2023 International
Conference on Medical Image Computing and Computer-Assisted
Intervention (pp. 456–468). Springer.
https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-031-12345-6_44
```

### Online Source

```
World Health Organization. (2023, March 15). Mental health and
COVID-19. https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/WHO-2019-nCoV-
Sci_Brief-Mental_health-2023.1
```

### Dataset

```
Smith, J. A. (2023). Decision-making under uncertainty dataset
[Data set]. Open Science Framework. https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/
ABCDEF
```

### Software

```
Bates, D., Mächler, M., Bolker, B., & Walker, S. (2015). Fitting
linear mixed-effects models using lme4. Journal of Statistical
Software, 67(1), 1–48. https://doi.org/10.18637/jss.v067.i01
```

### In-Text Citation Format

| Scenario | Format |
|---|---|
| One author | (Smith, 2023) |
| Two authors | (Smith & Jones, 2023) |
| Three+ authors | (Smith et al., 2023) |
| Author in sentence | Smith (2023) found that... |
| Two works, same author | (Smith, 2022, 2023) |
| Two works, different authors | (Jones, 2022; Smith, 2023) — alphabetical |
| Direct quote | (Smith, 2023, p. 45) |
| Group author | (World Health Organization [WHO], 2023); then (WHO, 2023) |

### Common APA Errors

1. ❌ Using "&" in text: "Smith & Jones (2023)" → ✅ "Smith and Jones (2023)"
2. ❌ Capitalizing article titles: → ✅ Sentence case
3. ❌ Missing DOI when available
4. ❌ Not including issue numbers when available
5. ❌ Using "et al." for 2–3 authors in text

## 2. IEEE Style

### Journal Article

```
J. A. Smith and B. C. Jones, "Neural mechanisms of decision-making
under uncertainty," J. Cogn. Neurosci., vol. 35, no. 4, pp. 567–589,
Apr. 2023, doi: 10.1162/jocn_a_01923.
```

### Conference Paper

```
L. Chen and R. Wang, "Attention-based models for medical image
segmentation," in Proc. Int. Conf. Med. Image Comput. Comput.-Assist.
Intervent. (MICCAI), Vancouver, Canada, 2023, pp. 456–468, doi:
10.1007/978-3-031-12345-6_44.
```

### Book

```
D. Kahneman, Thinking, Fast and Slow. New York, NY, USA: Farrar,
Straus and Giroux, 2011.
```

### Book Chapter

```
J. A. Smith, "Decision-making and the prefrontal cortex," in
Handbook of Cognitive Neuroscience, 2nd ed., B. C. Jones and D. Lee,
Eds. San Diego, CA, USA: Academic Press, 2023, pp. 234–256.
```

### Online Source

```
World Health Organization, "Mental health and COVID-19," Mar. 15,
2023. [Online]. Available: https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/
WHO-2019-nCoV-Sci_Brief-Mental_health-2023.1. [Accessed: Sep. 1,
2024].
```

### Technical Report / Standard

```
IEEE Standard for Floating-Point Arithmetic, IEEE Std 754-2019,
Aug. 2019, doi: 10.1109/IEEESTD.2019.8766229.
```

### In-Text Citation Format

IEEE uses numbered references in square brackets, ordered by first appearance:

| Scenario | Format |
|---|---|
| General citation | [1] |
| Multiple references | [1], [3], [5] or [1]–[5] |
| Reference in sentence | "As shown in [1], the results indicate..." |
| Multiple sources for same claim | [1], [3], [5] |
| Specific detail | [1, p. 45] or [1, Sec. III] |

### Common IEEE Errors

1. ❌ Listing authors in "Last, First" format → ✅ "First Last" format with initials
2. ❌ Using full journal names → ✅ Use IEEE abbreviations
3. ❌ Forgetting "in" before conference proceedings
4. ❌ Missing month and year for journal articles
5. ❌ Not including "doi:" prefix for DOIs

## 3. Nature Style

### Journal Article

```
Smith, J. A. & Jones, B. C. Neural mechanisms of decision-making
under uncertainty. J. Cogn. Neurosci. 35, 567–589 (2023).
```

### Article with 3+ Authors

```
Smith, J. A., Jones, B. C. & Lee, D. Neural mechanisms of
decision-making under uncertainty. J. Cogn. Neurosci. 35, 567–589
(2023).
```

### Article with 6+ Authors (first author + et al.)

```
Smith, J. A. et al. Neural mechanisms of decision-making under
uncertainty. J. Cogn. Neurosci. 35, 567–589 (2023).
```

### Book

```
Kahneman, D. Thinking, Fast and Slow (Farrar, Straus and Giroux,
2011).
```

### Book Chapter

```
Smith, J. A. Decision-making and the prefrontal cortex. In Handbook
of Cognitive Neuroscience (eds Jones, B. C. & Lee, D.) 234–256
(Academic Press, 2023).
```

### Conference Paper

```
Chen, L. & Wang, R. Attention-based models for medical image
segmentation. In Proc. Int. Conf. Med. Image Comput. Comput.-Assist.
Intervent. 456–468 (Springer, 2023).
```

### Preprint

```
Smith, J. A. & Jones, B. C. Neural mechanisms of decision-making
under uncertainty. Preprint at https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.01.01
(2023).
```

### In-Text Citation Format

| Scenario | Format |
|---|---|
| One author | (Smith, 2023) |
| Two authors | (Smith & Jones, 2023) |
| Three+ authors | (Smith et al., 2023) |
| Numbered reference | Superscript: result¹ |
| Multiple references | Superscript: result¹⁻³ |

### Common Nature Errors

1. ❌ Including article titles in reference list → ✅ Omit article titles (only include for books/chapters)
2. ❌ Including DOIs → ✅ Nature does not include DOIs
3. ❌ Full journal names → ✅ Use abbreviated journal names
4. ❌ Not using ampersand before last author → ✅ Use "&" not "and"
5. ❌ Including issue numbers → ✅ Omit issue numbers

## 4. AMA Style (American Medical Association, 11th Edition)

### Journal Article

```
Smith JA, Jones BC. Neural mechanisms of decision-making under
uncertainty. J Cogn Neurosci. 2023;35(4):567-589.
doi:10.1162/jocn_a_01923
```

### Six or More Authors (first 3 + et al.)

```
Smith JA, Jones BC, Lee D, et al. Neural mechanisms of
decision-making under uncertainty. J Cogn Neurosci.
2023;35(4):567-589. doi:10.1162/jocn_a_01923
```

### Book

```
Kahneman D. Thinking, Fast and Slow. Farrar, Straus and Giroux;
2011.
```

### Book Chapter

```
Smith JA. Decision-making and the prefrontal cortex. In: Jones BC,
Lee D, eds. Handbook of Cognitive Neuroscience. 2nd ed. Academic
Press; 2023:234-256.
```

### Online Source

```
World Health Organization. Mental health and COVID-19. Published
March 15, 2023. Accessed September 1, 2024.
https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/WHO-2019-nCoV-Sci_Brief-
Mental_health-2023.1
```

### In-Text Citation Format

AMA uses superscript numbered references, ordered by first appearance:

| Scenario | Format |
|---|---|
| General citation | Superscript: result¹ |
| Multiple references | Superscript: result¹,³,⁵ |
| Consecutive references | Superscript: result¹⁻³ |
| Author in sentence | Smith¹ reported that... |

### Common AMA Errors

1. ❌ Including periods after author initials → ✅ No periods in initials
2. ❌ Not abbreviating journal names → ✅ Use NLM abbreviations
3. ❌ Including "&" between authors → ✅ Use comma only
4. ❌ Using en-dash for page ranges → ✅ Use hyphen
5. ❌ Missing access date for online sources → ✅ Always include

## 5. Vancouver Style (ICMJE)

### Journal Article

```
Smith JA, Jones BC. Neural mechanisms of decision-making under
uncertainty. J Cogn Neurosci. 2023;35(4):567-589.
doi:10.1162/jocn_a_01923
```

### More Than Six Authors

```
Smith JA, Jones BC, Lee D, Garcia M, Patel R, Chen W, et al. Neural
mechanisms of decision-making under uncertainty. J Cogn Neurosci.
2023;35(4):567-589. doi:10.1162/jocn_a_01923
```

### Book

```
Kahneman D. Thinking, fast and slow. New York: Farrar, Straus and
Giroux; 2011.
```

### Book Chapter

```
Smith JA. Decision-making and the prefrontal cortex. In: Jones BC,
Lee D, editors. Handbook of cognitive neuroscience. 2nd ed. San
Diego: Academic Press; 2023. p. 234-256.
```

### In-Text Citation Format

| Scenario | Format |
|---|---|
| General citation | Numbered: (1) or superscript ¹ |
| Multiple references | (1,3,5) or ¹,³,⁵ |
| Consecutive | (1-3) or ¹⁻³ |

### Common Vancouver Errors

1. ❌ Not listing first 6 authors → ✅ List first 6, then "et al."
2. ❌ Using "Eds." instead of "editors" → ✅ Use "editor" or "editors"
3. ❌ Not including city of publication for books → ✅ Always include
4. ❌ Using semicolons between volume and issue → ✅ Use semicolons correctly

## 9. Format Conversion Strategies

### Converting Between Styles

| From → To | Key Changes |
|---|---|
| **APA → IEEE** | Change author format (Last, F.M. → F.M. Last); add quotation marks to article titles; change to numbered references; add "vol.", "no.", "pp."; abbreviate journal names |
| **APA → Nature** | Remove DOIs; remove article titles; abbreviate journal names; change author format; remove issue numbers; add year in parentheses at end |
| **APA → AMA** | Remove periods from initials; remove "&"; abbreviate journal names; change to numbered references; move year after journal name |
| **APA → Vancouver** | Similar to AMA; add city of publication for books; use "editors" instead of "Eds."; use "et al." after 6th author |
| **IEEE → APA** | Remove quotation marks from article titles; change to author-date system; spell out journal names; add DOIs |
| **Nature → APA** | Add article titles; add DOIs; spell out author first names; add issue numbers; change to alphabetical ordering |

### Automated Conversion Tools

| Tool | Supported Styles | Quality | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Zotero** | 10,000+ styles | Good | Best free option; style repository at zotero.org/styles |
| **Pandoc** | Many styles via CSL | Good | Best for batch conversion; command-line tool |
| **Citation Style Language (CSL)** | 9,000+ styles | Excellent | Underlying format for Zotero, Mendeley, Pandoc |
| **EndNote** | 7,000+ styles | Good | Requires paid license |
| **Mendeley** | 9,000+ styles | Good | Free; Elsevier-owned |
| **biblio-journal** | Limited | Variable | Online conversion; check output carefully |

### Manual Conversion Checklist

When converting between styles, verify:

- [ ] Author name format (Last, F.M. vs. F.M. Last vs. Last FM)
- [ ] Ampersand vs. "and" between authors
- [ ] Article title: sentence case vs. title case; present vs. omitted; quotes or no quotes
- [ ] Journal name: full vs. abbreviated
- [ ] Year placement: after author vs. after journal vs. at end
- [ ] Volume/issue format: italicized, abbreviated, or plain
- [ ] Page range format: en-dash vs. hyphen; "pp." or not
- [ ] DOI format: URL vs. "doi:" vs. omitted
- [ ] Reference ordering: alphabetical vs. numerical (by appearance)
- [ ] In-text citation: author-date vs. numbered vs. footnote
- [ ] Et al. threshold: 3 authors? 6 authors? 21 authors?
- [ ] Hanging indent vs. flush left in reference list

## 10. Common Formatting Errors by Style

### APA 7th
- Not using sentence case for article titles
- Missing DOIs (required when available)
- Using "&" in narrative citations (only use in parenthetical)
- Incorrect et al. usage (3+ authors from first citation)

### IEEE
- Using full author names instead of initials
- Not abbreviating journal names per IEEE standard
- Missing "in" before conference proceedings
- Forgetting "vol.", "no.", "pp." abbreviations

### Nature
- Including article titles (should be omitted for journal articles)
- Including DOIs (Nature omits them)
- Using full journal names (should abbreviate)
- Including issue numbers (Nature omits them)

### AMA
- Including periods in author initials
- Not using NLM journal abbreviations
- Missing access dates for online sources
- Using "&" between authors (use comma only)

### Vancouver
- Not listing first 6 authors before "et al."
- Using "Eds." instead of "editors"
- Missing city of publication for books
- Incorrect page number format
